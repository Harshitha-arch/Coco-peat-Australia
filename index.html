About Sri Amman Coirs

Sri Amman Coirs is a coconut fibre manufacturing company based in Tamil Nadu, India, operating continuously since 2008. For over 17 years, we have processed coconut husk into coco peat and coir-based products for domestic and international markets.

Our core strength lies in consistent raw material sourcing, controlled processing, and disciplined export documentation. This enables us to serve repeat buyers with stable specifications and dependable supply.

As part of our next phase of growth, we are establishing structured distribution channels for the Australian market, aligned with local buyer expectations and regulatory awareness.
